About MEDPRED

Welcome to MEDPRED, a web application designed to help you predict possible diseases based on symptoms.

How to Use

To use this web app, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Predict Disease page.
  2. Enter your symptoms (comma-separated) in the input field.
  3. Click on the "Predict" button.
  4. You will receive information about the predicted disease, including description, precautions, medications, workouts, and diets (if available).
